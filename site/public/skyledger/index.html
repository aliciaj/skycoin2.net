Skyledger: A platform powering the next generation of cryptocurrencies

Join the growing number of companies growing their business on our blockchain.

Skyledger gives developers a private blockchain that is fully customizable to their needs and equipped with all the benefits of Skycoin technology.

A launchpad for scalable, secure, and decentralized applications by 3rd parties—Skyledger is the solution for businesses seeking to adopt blockchain architecture.

Obelisk consensus algorithm

Skycoin developers spent seven years of research and development building out Obelisk, a consensus algorithm. Its web-of-trust mechanism allows for truly decentralized collective decision making.

Security and privacy

With better speed, better security, and better privacy, blockchain can finally reach its potential as a reliable, lightning-fast, versatile foundation for modern business.

Coins built on Skyledger get all the same benefits of Skycoin

No transaction fees

Skycoin has no mining and can’t be 51% attacked. Encrypted by default, it’s designed for security.

Near-instant transaction times

With no bottlenecks or fees, Skycoin’s two second transactions are faster than other cryptos and competitive with credit cards.

Low processing costs and energy requirements make Skycoin a truly sustainable and eco-friendly crypto.

Companies on Skyledger:

SPACO

Spaco is a decentralized service built on Skyledger, running storage and data transfer, a peer-to-peer encrypted Internet browser with built in anti-spam and versatile user-controlled feed filtering, as well as secure future applications in custom site-creation, shopping, games, video, and much more.

Solar Bankers

Solar Bankers provide a peer-to-peer market for electricity. Solar Bankers’ users generate solar electricity at their homes using patented solar devices. Users can then trade energy directly with their neighbors using SunChain and its currency, SunCoin.

Metalicoin

MetaliCoin is a cryptocurrency backed by real metals. Miners and metals enthusiasts can use MetaliCoin to buy and trade metals at deep discounts.

MyDailyLife (MDL) Talent Hub

MDL aims to build a trustchain for the $2.3 trillion entertainment industry. The MDL platform features a unique prosumer-based ecosystem marketplace and micro-KOL (Key Opinion Leader) partnership programs to raise revenue.
